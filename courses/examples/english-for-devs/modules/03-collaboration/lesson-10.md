# Agile & Standups

## Agile Terms

- **Sprint**: A time-boxed period to complete work.
- **Backlog**: A list of tasks to be done.
- **Ticket / Issue**: A specific task or bug report.
- **Epic**: A large body of work broken into smaller tickets.

## Daily Standup Vocabulary

- **Status update**: Sharing what you did and will do.
- **Blocker**: Something preventing you from working.
- **Deployed**: Code released to the production server.
- **WIP**: "Work In Progress".
